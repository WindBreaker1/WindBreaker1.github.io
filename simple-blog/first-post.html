Re-organizing Knowledge and Productivity Systems

Re-organizing such a system is a symptom of high-level procrastination.

It is high-level because the person involved in such procrastination isn't smoking weed or scrolling social media.

They are actually attempting to do a good thing, but by doing it repeatedly, it loses its meaning.

Ideally, a note-taking and knowledge-management system should last a lifetime. Yet a lot of people in the space are in the habit of frequent change.

Most of the time, this change is unwarranted and useless to the learning process.
